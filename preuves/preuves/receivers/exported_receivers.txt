Attack surface confirmation:
Drozer attack surface summary shows:
- 2 broadcast receivers exported

Analysis:
The main security concern is ShowDataReceiver because it is exported and has no permission protection.

An exported broadcast receiver without permission protection can receive broadcasts from other applications. If it does not validate the received action or sender, it may trigger unintended behavior.

The ProfileInstallReceiver is also exported, but it is protected by android.permission.DUMP, so the risk is lower.

Risk:
Unauthorized triggering of application logic through external broadcasts.

Severity:
- ShowDataReceiver: Medium
- ProfileInstallReceiver: Low

Recommendation:
- Set android:exported="false" for internal receivers.
- Add permission protection if the receiver must remain exported.
- Validate the received Intent action before executing logic.
- Ignore unexpected or malformed broadcasts.
