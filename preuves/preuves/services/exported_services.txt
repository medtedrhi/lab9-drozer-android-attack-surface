Attack surface confirmation:
Drozer attack surface summary shows:
- 1 service exported

Analysis:
The DownloadInvoiceService is exported and does not require any permission.

Because the service is exported, another application may be able to start it from outside the application. Since the service name suggests invoice downloading, this could expose internal background functionality if the service does not validate the caller or the received Intent.

Risk:
Unauthorized execution of background functionality.

Severity:
Medium

Recommendation:
- Set android:exported="false" if the service is only used internally.
- If external access is required, protect the service with a signature-level permission.
- Validate all incoming Intents before executing service logic.
- Reject null, malformed, or unexpected Intents.
